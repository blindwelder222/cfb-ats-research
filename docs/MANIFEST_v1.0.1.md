# Purpose

The College Football ATS Research Platform exists to build the most
comprehensive, transparent, reproducible, and statistically rigorous
research database possible for NCAA FBS Against the Spread (ATS)
analysis.

Every component of the Platform supports three objectives:

1.  Acquire complete and verifiable historical data.
2.  Build a standardized Master Database for long-term research.
3.  Produce reproducible statistical studies capable of identifying
    meaningful ATS tendencies.

The term **Platform** uses the canonical definition established by the
Constitution.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Mission

Transform raw CollegeFootballData (CFBD) API data into a fully
documented research platform that enables hypotheses to be tested
consistently, accurately, and reproducibly.

Methodology always takes precedence over conclusions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Vision

Create a permanent research platform that:

-   Automatically acquires historical and current FBS data.
-   Preserves immutable raw data.
-   Produces validated processed datasets.
-   Builds a unified Master Database.
-   Supports reusable Research Modules.
-   Enables future automation with minimal manual intervention.
