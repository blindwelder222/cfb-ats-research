This Constitution governs:

-   Project philosophy
-   Governance hierarchy
-   Core principles
-   Research standards
-   Software standards
-   Constitutional amendments

This Constitution does **not** define:

-   Database schemas

-   Field definitions
-   Statistical formulas

Those responsibilities are delegated to the appropriate canonical
documents.

# Preamble

This Constitution establishes the governing principles, standards, and
philosophy of the College Football ATS Research Platform.

For the purposes of this Constitution, the **Platform** refers to the
complete College Football ATS Research Platform, including governance,
acquisition, processing, storage, research, automation, and reporting
components.

This document is the supreme governing document of the project. All
other policies, standards, manuals, and implementation specifications
derive their authority from this Constitution.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Article I --- Mission

Build a comprehensive, reproducible research platform for NCAA FBS ATS
analysis using high-quality data, documented methodology, and
transparent statistical practices.

# Article II --- Core Principles

1.  Truth Before Conclusions
2.  Reproducibility
3.  Transparency
4.  Data Integrity
5.  Continuous Improvement

# Article III --- Governance

The Constitution is the highest governing document.

Subordinate canonical documents and responsibilities:

-   Project Manifest --- Project direction and objectives.
-   ATS Policy --- ATS methodology and policy.
-   Data Dictionary --- Canonical schema and field definitions.
-   Research Dictionary --- Canonical research terminology.
-   Data Acquisition Manual --- Acquisition workflow and operational
    procedures.
-   API Endpoint Reference --- Endpoint specifications and integration
    contracts.
-   Reference Tables --- Canonical reference datasets.

Conflicts are resolved in favor of the higher-order governing document.

# Article IV --- Data Governance

Platform data progresses through:

1.  Raw Data
2.  Processed Data
3.  Master Database
4.  Research Outputs

Raw data is preserved indefinitely.

# Article V --- Research Standards

Research shall document methodology, assumptions, sample sizes,
distinguish observation from interpretation, and avoid overstating
statistical significance.

# Article VI --- Software Standards

Software shall be version controlled, reproducible, documented, and
validated through proof-of-concept work before expansion.

# Bill of Definitions

The Data Dictionary and Research Dictionary are the canonical owners of
project terminology and definitions.

# Research Oath

Accuracy, transparency, and reproducibility take precedence over
convenience or desired outcomes.

# Constitutional Ratification

This Constitution serves as the governing framework of the College
Football ATS Research Platform. Amendments shall preserve historical
revisions through version control.
